CONTROLLERS:
Player One (Starts on Left Side):
	Controls with A(Left) D(Right) from the perspective of the root itself.

Player Two (Starts on Left Side):
	Controls with LEFT ARROW(Left) RIGHT ARROW(Right) from the perspective of the root itself.

Pressing a key multiple times will tighten the turning angle.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

INSTRUCTIONS:
Two plants fight for control with their roots.
Do not hit the edges of the screen nor the current roots(the previous ones are just visual).
Whenever you hear the sound prompt, be ready as the roots will speed up.Â´
